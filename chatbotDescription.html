The FAQ ChatBot is a web-based question-answering system designed to mimic an interactive Frequently Asked Questions assistant. Built with Flask on the backend and HTML/CSS on the frontend, this project allows users to input questions in natural language and receive relevant answers from a curated FAQ dataset.

At its core, the chatbot leverages TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to transform text into numerical vectors, and uses cosine similarity to measure the similarity between the userâ€™s question and existing FAQs. The answer with the highest similarity score is returned, making it capable of understanding user intent even when the exact wording doesn't match.

This approach enables the chatbot to deliver meaningful and contextually accurate answers, making it a lightweight yet powerful solution for organizations looking to automate customer support, onboarding FAQs, or internal knowledge bases.
